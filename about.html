History Dandelion Place

7th heaven,Gold palace,peace satirum,hover joy and many more names people gave it. Dandelion plce started out humbly in 1943 as a small restaurant which served the minister during colonial period along kwale road.

We served as a a restaurant with no accomodation and offered local dishes at the time but with time weve grown into a big 5star hotel offering all type of foods all over the world

we are humbled from where we come from and thank God for where we are. With your continued ssupport i believe we will go further than this
